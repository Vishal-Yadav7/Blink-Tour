Popular destination

Immerse yourself in the colorful tapestry of India. From the majestic Gateway of India to the bustling streets of Delhi, every corner tells a story waiting to be discovered.

  • Maldives

    Maldives

    The Maldives, a tropical haven of luxury resorts, stunning beaches, and vibrant marine life, promises an unforgettable escape into paradise.

  • Jaipur, Rajasthan

    Hawa Mahal

    FHawa Mahal, Jaipur, a stunning red and pink sandstone palace adjoining the City Palace.

  • Gujarat

    Statue Of Unity

    Statue of Unity in Gujarat, India, stands at 182 meters, honoring Vallabhbhai Patel, India's first deputy prime minister.

  • Delhi

    Lotus Temple

    The Lotus Temple in New Delhi, India, is a Baháʼí House of Worship known for its lotus-like shape, opened in 1986. It welcomes all, irrespective of faith.

  • Delhi

    Red Fort

    The Red Fort in Delhi, India, was commissioned by Emperor Shah Jahan in 1639 as his new capital's main residence.

  • Uttar Pradesh

    Varanasi

    Varanasi, in Uttar Pradesh, India, is a spiritual hub with ancient roots, attracting Hindu pilgrims to its sacred Ganges River for rituals and bathing.
